ABOUT US

Safeguarding Our Environment, One Hydro-Kinetic System At A Time

At Norweco of New York, we are dedicated to creating sustainable wastewater solutions that safeguard Long Island's natural beauty. As the exclusive distributor of Norweco products, we specialize in eco-friendly systems like the Hydro-Kinetic, which reduces nitrogen pollution and protects our vital water resources. With over six decades of industry experience, we are proud to be at the forefront of preserving our island's environment for future generations.
